Troubleshoot

Audio is not working on mobile device.

If any other device mic is on, turn off the mic of that device otherwise try reconnecting to the meeting. If the trouble continues, reach us at help@acumeet.com.

There was echo is my AcuMeet meeting.

Try switching off the mic/audio or try to leave meeting and reconnect. If the trouble continues, reach us at help@acumeet.com.

My webcam/camera is not working properly.

Try switching off the camera/webcam or try to leave meeting and reconnect. If the trouble continues, reach us at help@acumeet.com.

I cannot share my screen in meeting.

Try sharing screen after switching off screen share once again. If the trouble continues, reach us at help@acumeet.com.

I was not able to join a meeting using the provided link.

Your internet connection might not be stable. If the trouble continues, reach us at help@acumeet.com.
